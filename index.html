His work sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence, technology policy, and operations, with a focus on deploying AI systems that perform reliably in high-stakes environments such as governance, national security, geopolitics, healthcare, and global markets.

He brings founder experience from multiple AI startups, has worked within VC-backed technology companies, and has contributed to AI and tech policy initiatives shaping responsible adoption. Methodology

  • All events are normalized to a unified schema
  • Deduplication using content hashing and clustering
  • Source attribution and timestamps for every event
  • Confidence scores for unverified OSINT signals
  • Automated filtering of non-English and .edu sources

Security & Privacy

  • No user accounts or authentication required
  • No tracking or data collection
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • SSRF protection on all ingestion endpoints
  • Content Security Policy headers enforced

Disclaimer

!ntellibot aggregates information from public sources. We do not verify the accuracy of third-party content. OSINT signals are labeled as unverified and should be independently confirmed before acting upon. This tool is for informational purposes only.
